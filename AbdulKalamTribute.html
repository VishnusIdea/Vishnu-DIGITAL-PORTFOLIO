Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Biography

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, widely known as Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, was an eminent scientist and the 11th President of India. He was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, India. Dr. Kalam played a pivotal role in India's civilian space program and military missile development. He was a visionary leader and a source of inspiration for millions around the world.
